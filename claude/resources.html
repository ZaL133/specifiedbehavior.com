Resources

A curated collection of courses, tools, and communities to help you learn about AI and machine learning.

Learning Resources

  • Machine Learning by Andrew Ng — Coursera A comprehensive introduction to machine learning and statistical pattern recognition. One of the most widely recommended starting points.
  • Kaggle Learn Hands-on learning with real datasets, notebooks, and competitions. Great for practical experience.
  • DeepLearning.AI Comprehensive AI education founded by Andrew Ng, covering everything from neural networks to MLOps.
  • Google AI Education Resources and tools from Google to learn the core concepts of machine learning.
  • fast.ai A practical, top-down approach to deep learning. Free courses and a strong community.

AI Tools & Services

  • OpenAI Creator of GPT-4 and ChatGPT. Offers API access for building AI-powered applications.
  • Anthropic AI safety company and creator of Claude. Strong focus on safe, interpretable AI systems.
  • Google AI Platform A suite of services to build, train, and deploy machine learning models at scale.
  • AWS AI Services Amazon's collection of AI services including Rekognition, Transcribe, Comprehend, and Bedrock.
  • Microsoft Azure AI Azure's AI and cognitive services suite, including Azure OpenAI for enterprise GPT access.
  • Hugging Face The hub for open-source AI models. Thousands of pretrained models for NLP, vision, audio, and more.

AI Design & Creative Tools

  • Runway ML Code-free AI tools for creators — video generation, image editing, and more.
  • Artbreeder Mix and evolve images using AI. A fun tool for exploring generative art.
  • Midjourney Text-to-image AI that produces stunning, artistic visuals from natural language prompts.

Blogs & Communities

  • Google AI Blog Latest research and updates from Google's AI teams.
  • /r/MachineLearning A large community on Reddit discussing and sharing the latest in machine learning research.
  • AI on Medium A broad collection of AI articles from practitioners, researchers, and enthusiasts.
  • KDnuggets A leading site covering AI, analytics, big data, data science, and machine learning news.
  • Import AI (newsletter) Jack Clark's weekly newsletter covering the most important AI research and policy developments.
